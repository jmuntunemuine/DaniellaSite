SOCIAL MEDIA AND YOUTH PRIVACY - HOME

Thousands of people around the world use social media and a significant amount of them are youth. The purpose of this report was to educate youth on social privacy. A spreadsheet and questionnaire were used to collect and analysis information. In the light of the findings, it is evident that people who did not make use of privacy settings were prone to privacy issues. This research will in light the youth on how to protect themselves and be knowledgeable on social media privacy.

In this digital age, Technology had given people the freedom to share personal information and post pictures on social media platforms, perform financial transactions like online shopping, connect their devices to the internet and many more but there were some potential threats along with them. The main one being privacy issues whereby people s privacy was not being respected online particularly on social media. Some social media sites sold personal information on their users to advertisers to create personalized ads for businesses based on their preferences and what they view without their permission other problems include identity theft and social media platforms being hacked or taken over by people which led to personal information being leaked to the public.
